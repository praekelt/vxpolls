QA via Gtalk

While the SMS provider and gateway are still in the process of being setup, the SMS application can in the mean time be tested by using GTalk or any other Jabber/XMPP based chat client by following these instructions:

  • Add your configured Gtalk address as a contact to your Gtalk contact list.
  • Initiate the conversation by sending something random like "hi"
  • The menu will return and ask your first question. It is configured to ask questions in batches.
